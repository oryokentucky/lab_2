Campus Story: The Long Walk from Batu Pahat Mall to UTHM

One sunny afternoon, my friends and I decided to take on an adventure we had never imagined. After a fun day at Batu Pahat Mall, we suddenly thought, "Why don’t we walk back to UTHM?" It sounded like a wild idea, but that’s what made it exciting!

The Journey Begins

We set out with nothing but our backpacks and a few bottles of water. The sun was just beginning to set, painting the sky with hues of orange and pink. We figured it would take a few hours, but we were ready for the challenge.

Unexpected Encounters

As we walked along the main road, we encountered local vendors, friendly passersby, and even a stray cat that followed us for a short distance. Every mile felt like a mini-adventure. We shared stories, laughed, and took countless photos to capture the journey.

The Tiring Middle Stretch

Around halfway, fatigue started to set in. The excitement turned to silent determination as our steps got slower. We took breaks by the roadside, watching cars zoom past, wondering if anyone else had ever attempted this crazy walk.

The Final Push

When UTHM's lights finally appeared in the distance, our energy surged. We quickened our pace, feeling like we had accomplished something amazing. As we reached the university gates, we all cheered, exhausted but proud. That walk from Batu Pahat Mall to UTHM became a story we’d never forget, one we’d share with friends for years to come.
